About the AAU Students Helpdesk

The AAU Students Helpdesk is a dedicated platform designed to assist students of Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma, with their academic and administrative needs. This system allows students to register, log in, and submit complaints related to portal access, payment issues, course registration, examination schedules, and more. The goal is to provide a user-friendly interface to streamline communication between students and university support staff, ensuring timely resolution of issues.
